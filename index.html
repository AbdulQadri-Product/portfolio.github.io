About Me

Turning ambiguity
into clarity.

I'm a Business Analyst and Product Manager based in Nigeria. I co-founded Odyssey Technologies in late 2025 and have since built process discipline, product strategy, and delivery governance on a live, pre-launch product — Mocksy.

My work sits across two disciplines: on the BA side, I handle requirements elicitation, stakeholder management, process mapping, and documentation. On the PM side, I own the product vision, roadmap, go-to-market strategy, and the decisions that shape what gets built and when.

I'm a final-year Law student at the University of Ilorin — a background that sharpens my ability to think precisely, structure arguments, and navigate compliance and contractual work. Available for remote and hybrid BA and PM roles.
